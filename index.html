Happy Valentine's Day

Dear momo,

I can't believe it has been more than 2 years since we first met. Valentine's day is coming up and I was going through all our memories together thinking about what would be a beautiful thing to get you. While going through these old photos I couldn't stop smiling the whole time. I realized what could be more beautiful than to take you through this journey we have been through again. To take you through how we fell in love deeper and deeper with every passing day for the past 2 years.

The day we first met

I can't believe this is where we first met. The first time I saw you was in between a sea of people, but as soon as I saw you I couldn't take my eyes off you. I don't regret not taking a picture that day, because everything about that day is in my head, clear as if it just happened yesterday.

The day you told me your feelings

I still remember the day you said this to me. Probably the happiest day of my life. I was so happy I took a screenshot of it and kept looking at it for days after.

The day we first met after we started dating

The 8 months I didn't see you after we first met felt like 8 decades. I remember showing up at your building and seeing you walk towards me and all I could think was - Wow! She is the most beautiful woman I have ever seen, and she is my girlfriend! I was so shocked that I went in for a handshake instead of a hug!

Our first trip

I still remember how happy you were when we went to the hotel, you couldn't stop smiling. It was a short trip but it became the best trip of my life...atleast until the next one ;)

Our first new year's

We spent 2 days at the Orchid for new year's. I think we left the room only twice the whole time we were there. We just enjoyed each other's company so much that we didn't realize when 2 days went by. And we had our first new year's kiss!

Goa trip!

The first long trip we went on. And it was the best! Be it walking on the beach hand in hand or kayaking or exploring the streets on the scooty, I just had the best time with you. Even just laying in bed at the hotel watching a movie was memorable.

Our first concert

I was so worried when I was planning this surprise. I didn't know if you would like it. But I saw the look of surprise and happiness on your face as soon as I showed you that wristband and I knew you loved it! We didn't know a lot of the songs he sang but just vibing with you alongside me and seeing you happy and excited made it so memorable for me!

Lonavala trip

After the concert we went to Lonavala, the hotel with the best view I have seen so far. But that view still paled in comparison to the smile you had on your face from the entire weekend. It was a perfect weekend!

Meeting the parents

I didn't think I could meet anyone as sweet as you ever in my life. But then I met your family. Everyone treated me like I was part of the family. Your parents treated me like their own son.

Coldplay!

I remember you telling me it was your dream to attend a Coldplay concert. When they announced their India tour we were both so excited. I could tell you were heartbroken when we didn't get the tickets. But then you had that big smile on your face when we got the Abu Dhabi tickets. I still remember the tears in your eyes when they played Fix You. I am so lucky to have experienced that with the love of my life.

Dubai trip

This entire trip was like a dream. You were so excited and happy every place we went. You were like a small child taking pictures of everything with a huge smile. I loved it! I don't think I am still over this trip. I don't know how every trip we go on becomes the best trip of my life, but again, this is the best trip of my life; until next time!

Every moment I have spent with you has been the best time of my life. I can't thank you enough for the last 2 years. And I promise you that we are going to have a lifetime of these happy memories together!

Forever Yours,

Aditya ❤️
